## 🎯 BEST PRACTICES FOR QUESTIONS

✅ **DO:**
- Be specific: "How to control fruit borer in tomato?" (not "pest control")
- Ask one topic: "Onion diseases?" (then ask about treatment separately)
- Use crop name: "Cabbage fertilizer" (not "leafy vegetable fertilizer")
- Mention problem clearly: "Tomato leaves turning yellow"
- Give context: "What to grow after potato?"

❌ **DON'T:**
- Ask multiple topics at once
- Use unclear crop names
- Ask non-farming questions
- Ask for real-time weather
- Mix languages in one question
