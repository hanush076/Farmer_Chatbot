💡 EXPERT TIPS FOR ASKING QUESTIONS:

  ✅ DO:
    • Be specific: "Fruit borer in tomato?" (not just "pests")
    • Mention crop name: "Onion disease?" (not "vegetable disease")
    • Ask one topic: Ask about treatment after diagnosis
    • Give context: "Yellow leaves on tomato" (not just "problem")
    • Use clear language: "When to harvest?" (not "when ready?")

  ❌ DON'T:
    • Ask multiple crops at once
    • Use unclear crop names
    • Ask non-farming questions
    • Ask for real-time weather
    • Mix unrelated topics
